Kith x Columbia

Client

Kith / Columbia Sportswear

Services

Creative Direction, Production, Cinematography

Year

2024

VLACOVISION captured the essence of the Kith x Columbia collaboration, a groundbreaking fusion of street culture and outdoor performance. This project celebrates the intersection of two iconic brands, showcasing how premium streetwear design meets technical outdoor apparel to create something entirely new and compelling.

Our visual approach emphasized the collection's dual nature—equally at home on urban streets and mountain trails. We crafted scenes that highlight the versatility and craftsmanship of the collaboration, featuring authentic moments that demonstrate how the collection seamlessly transitions between city and wilderness. The cinematography balances Kith's refined aesthetic sensibility with Columbia's heritage of outdoor innovation.

Through careful styling and dynamic locations, we've created a narrative that speaks to a generation that refuses to choose between style and function. The project showcases how this collaboration reimagines what's possible when two distinct brand philosophies unite, resulting in apparel that performs as beautifully as it looks.
