Introducing GT

Client

GT Bicycles

Services

Creative Direction, Production, Cinematography

Year

2023

VLACOVISION partnered with GT Bicycles to create a launch campaign celebrating their legendary mountain biking heritage. This project honors GT's decades of innovation in BMX and mountain biking while introducing their latest designs to a new generation of riders who demand performance, durability, and style on every trail.

Our cinematography captures the pure joy and technical capability that GT bikes deliver across diverse terrain. We documented riders pushing the limits on challenging singletrack, catching air on jump lines, and carving through technical descents—all while showcasing the engineering excellence and design innovation that has made GT a trusted name in cycling for over four decades.

Through dynamic visuals and compelling rider stories, we've created content that bridges GT's storied past with their innovative future. The project emphasizes the brand's continued commitment to creating bikes that inspire confidence and enable riders to progress their skills, whether they're tackling their first trail or competing at the highest levels of the sport.
