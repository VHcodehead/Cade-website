Lost Summer

Client

Independent

Services

Production, Direction, Cinematography

Year

2023

VLACOVISION presents "Lost Summer," a documentary exploration of adventure, friendship, and the pursuit of perfect moments during the fleeting warmth of summer. This project captures the essence of seasonal freedom—those precious months when long days, warm weather, and good company create the conditions for unforgettable experiences and lasting memories.

Our documentary approach followed a group of friends as they chased summer across diverse landscapes and adventures. We documented spontaneous road trips, campfire conversations under star-filled skies, early morning surf sessions, mountain biking through golden afternoon light, and the bittersweet awareness that these perfect days can't last forever. The cinematography balances epic landscape shots with intimate moments that capture the bonds formed through shared experiences.

Through evocative visuals and authentic storytelling, we've created a film that's both a time capsule and a reminder to make the most of every summer. The project explores themes of youth, friendship, and the importance of choosing experiences over comfort—a love letter to the adventures we take and the people we share them with during those irreplaceable summer months.
