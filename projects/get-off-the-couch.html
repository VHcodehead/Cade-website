Get Off The Couch

Client

Independent

Services

Creative Direction, Production, Post-Production

Year

2023

VLACOVISION created "Get Off The Couch" as a motivational call-to-action for the outdoor lifestyle community. This project challenges viewers to break free from screens and comfort zones, trading Netflix marathons for real adventures in the mountains, trails, and wild places that are waiting just beyond the front door.

Our storytelling approach juxtaposes the mundane routine of indoor life with the vibrant, energizing experiences that await outside. We documented everyday people making the choice to prioritize outdoor experiences—early morning surf sessions before work, post-office trail runs, weekend camping trips, and the transformative effect these activities have on mental and physical well-being.

Through inspiring visuals and relatable narratives, we've created content that resonates with anyone who's ever felt the pull of adventure but struggled to break free from inertia. The project serves as both inspiration and gentle provocation: the outdoors is calling, your body was made to move, and the best day of your week is probably waiting outside right now—so what are you waiting for?
