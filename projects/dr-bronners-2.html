Dr. Bronner's

Client

Dr. Bronner's

Services

Production, Direction, Post-Production

Year

2023

VLACOVISION returned to Dr. Bronner's to document their "All-One" philosophy in action, taking viewers beyond the product to explore the real-world impact of their ethical business practices. This project showcases how their commitment to social and environmental responsibility creates positive change throughout their entire supply chain and beyond.

Our storytelling approach took us to the source—documenting fair trade partnerships with coconut and palm oil farmers, organic agriculture initiatives, and community projects that Dr. Bronner's supports worldwide. We captured the human faces behind the brand's mission, showing how ethical sourcing and profit-sharing create sustainable livelihoods and strengthen communities across the globe.

Through authentic documentary-style cinematography and compelling personal stories, we've created a project that demonstrates how business can be a powerful vehicle for positive change. The film illustrates Dr. Bronner's belief that we're all connected—that supporting farmers, communities, and the environment isn't just good ethics, it's the foundation of a truly sustainable business model that benefits everyone.
